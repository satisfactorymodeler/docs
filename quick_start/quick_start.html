Satisfactory Modeler

Quick Start

To add machines, double click or right click the background to open the Recipe Chooser.
Select a recipe from the right list, this can be filtered at the top, or select a specialty machine from the left list.

Once added, machines can be deleted by double clicking or right clicking on them to bring up their menu.

Connect machines together by dragging a connection from an output to an input or an input to an output.
These connections can be deleted by redragging them or right clicking the part label.

Machines can have limits placed on the number of them in the field at the bottom of the machine.

Miners and the AWESOME Sink are represented by the number of parts per minute by default, other machines are represented by the number of machines.

This tool will automatically calculate the max flow of parts without going over any of the entered limits, accurately modeling how splitters and mergers will flow.


Tips

  • Dragging a connection onto the background will open the recipe chooser filtered to valid recipes.
  • Almost everything has a hover tooltip for more info. Hover over numbers to see the exact amount in fraction form.
  • Limits can be entered as whole numbers, decimals, fractions (including mixed numbers). Ex: 1.2, 1 1/5, 6/5, even 4.32/3.6
  • This tool calculates exact numbers, no rounding, so be precise in your inputs.
  • Standard keybinds for undo/redo.
  • Drag the background to pan. Use the scroll wheel to scroll in and out.
  • Click a machine or right click drag a box to select machines. Keybinds to cut, copy, paste, and delete the selection.
  • Open the menu in the top right for lots of options.
